<h1 align="center">PlumJuice HPC Competitive Team</h1>
<p align="center"><i>It's like a fruit salad 🥗</i></p>

The **PlumJuice Competitive Team** is a team of students from [Sapienza University](https://uniroma1.it) that specializes in HPC competitions. The original members gathered during a multicore programming course, and decided to stick together and participate to the IndySCC (now SCC Connect) competition during 2025. After competing in it, the team sticked around and aims to participate to more competitions in the future. Curious to meet them? Here is a nice family picture:
