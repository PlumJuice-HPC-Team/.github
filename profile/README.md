<h1 align="center">PlumJuice HPC Competitive Team</h1>

<p align="center">
  <i>It's like a fruit salad 🥗</i>
</p>

<p>
  The <b>PlumJuice Competitive Team</b> is a group of students from 
  <a href="https://uniroma1.it">Sapienza University</a>, specializing in HPC competitions.
  The team originated during a multicore programming course and was formed to participate in the
  <b>IndySCC 2025</b> competition (now <i>SCC Connect</i>).
  After competing, the group continued to collaborate with the goal of taking part in future HPC challenges.
</p>
